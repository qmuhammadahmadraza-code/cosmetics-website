Our Story

A small idea about honest beauty — grown into something real.

Where it all began

In 2019, two sisters sat in a small kitchen trying to figure out why every moisturizer they picked up had a 40-ingredient list they couldn't pronounce.

They weren't chemists. They were just two women tired of not knowing what they were putting on their skin — and stubborn enough to do something about it.

They started Verdure with a single serum, a kitchen blender, and a vision: beauty products made from things that grow from the earth. Nothing hidden, nothing harmful.

How we make things differently

Every Verdure product starts with the active ingredient — not the marketing angle. We identify a skin concern, research the best plant-based solution, then build a formula around it.

We partner with certified sustainable farms across Europe and South Asia. Every ingredient is traceable, every batch is third-party tested before it reaches you.

No filler ingredients. No proprietary blends hiding behind vague names. Just honest formulas — and the reason each ingredient is there.
